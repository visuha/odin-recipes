Zucchini Cheddar Pancakes

Description

Six simple ingredients come together for a deliciously savory zucchini pancake, featuring Cheddar cheese and onions mixed into the batter.
You can jazz these up however you'd like; some reviewers suggest marinara sauce for dipping, while others added garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup grated onion
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup biscuit baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, as needed

Steps

  1. Mix zucchini, Cheddar cheese, and onion together in a bowl. Stir eggs and biscuit mix into zucchini mixture until well combined.
  2. Heat vegetable oil on the griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Scoop about 1/4 cup batter onto heated griddle; cook until pancakes are browned, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Repeat with remaining batter.
